## 🌍 Our Mission
 
> *"We are here to spread awareness across the entire farming community — conventional, organic, small-scale, large-scale, new, and experienced. Every farmer, every crop, every field deserves access to knowledge."*
 
Farmer's Smart Assistant is a **farming awareness and intelligence platform** built to democratize agricultural knowledge across India. Whether a farmer grows wheat in Punjab, rice in West Bengal, or vegetables in Uttarakhand — this platform speaks to them, guides them, and grows with them.

## ✨ Core Features
 
### 🤖 AI-Powered Smart Crop Advisor
Hyper-personalized recommendations for **any farming method** — conventional, organic, mixed, or hydroponic — powered by Claude AI. Ask anything, get expert-level answers instantly.
 
### 📢 Farming Awareness Hub
A dedicated section spreading awareness on:
- Best farming practices across all crop types
- Soil health and conservation techniques
- Water management and rainwater harvesting
- Seasonal farming calendar for every Indian region
- Modern vs traditional farming — what works when
 
### 🌦️ Real-Time Weather Intelligence
Live weather data powers irrigation, harvesting, and sowing decisions. Farmers get proactive alerts before extreme weather hits their crops.
 
### 🗣️ Multilingual for Every Indian Farmer
Full support for **Hindi and regional Indian languages** — because the farmer in rural Uttarakhand deserves the same quality of knowledge as anyone else.
 
### 🏛️ Government Scheme Discovery
Automatically surfaces PM-KISAN, Soil Health Cards, crop insurance, state subsidies, and 20+ schemes tailored to the farmer's profile. No more leaving money on the table.
 
### 📱 Works Everywhere — Even Offline
Progressive Web App (PWA) designed for **low-connectivity rural India**. Install it on any Android phone, works without stable internet.
 
### 🎓 Farmer's Knowledge Library
A growing, AI-curated library of farming knowledge — from basic soil preparation to advanced pest management — written in simple, jargon-free language accessible to every farmer regardless of education level.
 
### 🌱 Organic Farming Transition Guide
A dedicated guided pathway for farmers wanting to shift to organic farming — step by step, season by season, with zero overwhelm.
